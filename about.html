About me

Kalina is from the town of Marblehead, Massachusetts and is in her second year at Fordham University. Her connection with the arts began at a young age as she played the piano and learned music theory starting in late elementary school. In high school she began painting at a local art studio and fell in love with it. This led her to develop a deeper involvement in the arts as a whole. She developed further interests in film, photography, and fashion history and theory which she hope to integrate in future work.
